Recommender systems

Nowadays, recommender systems have been widely used in various real-world services like Amazon, Netflix, YouTube, Spotify, and so on. The recommender system is a subtask of information retrieval to predict the “rating” or “preference” of an item for a target user. Our research project focuses on designing and developing novel recommender models that consider several factors such as accuracy, scalability, time-awareness, interpretability, and diversity.

Natural language processing (NLP)

NLP is a branch of artificial intelligence that deals with the interaction between computers and humans using natural language. The ultimate goal of NLP is to let computers read, understand, and generate human-level languages. Our research goal is to develop the model for natural language understanding (NLU) as well as natural language generation (NLG).

Machine/Deep learning algorithm

Machine learning (ML) is the study of algorithms and models to provide systems the ability to learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. Our research goal is to design more accurate and reliable models for supervised/unsupervised learning as well as reinforcement learning.
